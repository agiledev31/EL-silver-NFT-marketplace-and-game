Trade Requirements
Create a Trade Posting
Before creating a trade please go through our Terms of Service and the Online Sale guide.
  • Rules and requirements while posting a trade
  • For your post to display you need to have Silver in your wallet.
  • The user who posts a trade is charged 1% of the total trade volume for each completed trade.
  • Once a trade is opened the price is final, except when there is a clear mistake in the pricing.
  • You are not allowed to buy or sell Silver on behalf of someone else (brokering).
  • You may only send and receive payment using accounts that are registered in your own name. Third party payments are not allowed.
  • You must provide your payment details in the advertisement or in the trade chat.
  • All communication must happen on the LoL Silver platform.
  • Payment methods marked High Risk have a significant risk of fraud. Be careful and always ID verify your trading partners when using high risk payment methods.
